What is the nervous system?

The nervous system is the part of the body that controls voluntarily had involuntarily actions. It consists of two parts, the central nervous system and the peripheral nervous system. The CNS contains the brain and spinal cord while the PNS contains motor neurons; which control voluntary actions. It also contains the autonomic nervous system. The nervous system contains a special type of cell, neurons; nerve cells. These neurons send signals rapidly to other cells, and send actions which allow your body to perform voluntary and involuntary actions.
