Symptoms

Am I likely to have an intracerebral hemmorhage?

If an intracerebral hemmorhage occurs, then brain functions die, which cause symptoms listed below. As functions stop functioning, then simple functions like moving an arm, or even swallowing.

Do I have it?

There are any symptoms of brain bleed. They can include:

  • Headaches
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Trouble swallowing
  • Sudden weakness in arms, face or legs
  • Loss of conciousness
  • Seizures
  • Strokes
  • Confusion
  • Paralysis in the face
